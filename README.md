🎯 Game Rules

    Goal: The first player to reach exactly 70 points wins

    Rolling Dice: Each time you roll, the dice number is added to your current round score

    Number 1: If you roll a 1, you lose your turn and your current round score resets to zero

    Hold: Press the Hold button to add your current round score to your total score

    70+ Rule: If your score exceeds 70, your current round score is burned and the turn passes to your opponent

    Game Start: The inactive player starts the game with 5 points

✨ Features

    🎲 Realistic dice animation with actual dots

    🎉 Confetti animation for winner celebration

    📱 Fully responsive design

    🔄 Player status display (Playing / Waiting)

    💬 Visual alerts for the 70+ rule
